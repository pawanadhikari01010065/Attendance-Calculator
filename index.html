How to Use the Attendance Calculator

Enter total classes held and classes attended. Move the slider to choose your target attendance percentage. Click Calculate to see your attendance status.

Attendance Formula

Attendance Percentage = (Classes Attended / Total Classes) × 100

What is a Bunk Calculator?

A bunk calculator helps students determine how many classes they can miss while maintaining their target attendance percentage.
